Alcatraz Fashion

Alcatraz Fashion is a front-end ecommerce-style project built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The site presents a fashion brand with hero sliders for featured products (including hoodies, jackets, and tees), clear navigation, pricing highlights, testimonials, and a contact section—structured so visitors can browse collections and understand delivery and quality promises.

I focused on layout, typography, and responsive behavior so the page feels like a real storefront: strong product headlines, readable descriptions, and call-to-action buttons that guide users to read more or get in touch. This was an early full-page build that helped me practice component-style sections and consistent styling across the whole experience.
